MY PET

My pet is a dog whose name happens to be Snoopy.

Snoopy has got some various characteristics or features with which one can use to identify him.

These features include:

  • He is white in color with dark black spots
  • Has a bushy tail
  • Has a short tongue which he likes sticking out
  • He is a dog who is always walking around or playing

However, there are some things which Snoopy does once you annoy him:

  • He barks alot
  • He grins his teeth and growls
  • If he is too angry,he may chase after you and bite you!

Snoopy is a sweet dog but you have to handle him catiously!
